Selected Projects

Neuroplastic Neural Network

Novel framework that explores how biologically inspired neuroplasticity might improve artificial deep learning. Includes manually designed Multi-layered Perceptrons and Convolutional Blocks with integrated Hebbian Learning, structural plasticity mechanics, and self-modulated learning.

Multi-Layer Perceptron Convolutional Neural Network Neuromorphic AI

Neural Connectome Project

Implemented a LIF SNN from scratch and OpenWorm datasets. Modeled all 302 neurons and 5100+ synapses of C. elegans to emulate complete connectome dynamics through simulating chemo-taxis.

Spiking Neural Network Reinforcement Learning Neuromorphic AI

PulseTrace

Agentic AI agent capable of scraping social media, and summarizing sentiment through FAISS + BM25 with rerankers, and a RAG pipeline. Optional PDF briefing and AI corpus integrated with application following BYOK. Beats Last30Days on nDCG@5 by 38%, and on precision@5 and mean norm by roughly 14%.

LangGraph Hybrid RAG Model Context Protocol Agentic AI
